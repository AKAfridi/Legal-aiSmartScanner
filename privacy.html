Privacy Policy

App: AI Smart Scanner · Provider: MAFCoders
Effective date: 6 July 2026 · Last updated: 6 July 2026 · Contact: maafcoders@gmail.com

Summary. AI Smart Scanner is privacy-first. Your documents are stored on your device by default, and text recognition (OCR) runs on-device. We use no advertising, no analytics, no crash-reporting, and no third-party tracking, and we do not sell your personal information. We do not operate a server that stores your documents. Some optional features send data to third parties only when you use them — explained below.

1. Who We Are

AI Smart Scanner is a document scanner and PDF/AI toolkit provided by MAFCoders ("we", "us", "our"). For any privacy question or request, contact maafcoders@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Account information (only if you sign in). Sign-in is optional and is used mainly to link and restore your subscription. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive — through Google Firebase Authentication — your name and email address and a unique account identifier (Firebase User ID). You can use the App without signing in.
  • Subscription & purchase information. When you purchase a subscription, our billing provider RevenueCat, together with the Apple App Store or Google Play, processes the purchase and provides us with your subscription status and an app-user identifier. We do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • Push & device-integrity tokens. If you enable notifications, Firebase Cloud Messaging generates a device push token used to deliver announcements. Firebase App Check generates a device-integrity token to verify requests come from a genuine installation of the App.
  • Documents & content you create. Documents you scan, import, create, or edit — including extracted text, titles, folders, categories, signatures, and annotations — are stored on your device. This content leaves your device only when you actively use an AI feature or a cloud-backup feature (see Sections 5 and 7).

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No advertising networks.
  • No analytics or usage-tracking SDKs.
  • No crash-reporting SDKs.
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.

4. Device Permissions

  • Camera — to scan documents and capture images (processed on your device).
  • Photo library / media (images) — to import images and, when you choose, save exported files.
  • Notifications — to show subscription/trial reminders and product announcements. You can disable them in your device settings.
  • Biometrics / Face ID / fingerprint — to lock and unlock the Secure Folder locally. Biometric data never leaves your device and is handled by the operating system.
  • Internet / network — required for AI features, sign-in, purchases, notifications, and optional cloud backup.
  • Microphone (iOS) — a microphone usage description is declared by the underlying camera component; the App captures still images only and does not record or transmit audio.

5. AI Processing (Google Gemini)

When you use an AI feature (for example, summarize, chat about a document, smart rename, extract data, classify, translate, or "more accurate" text extraction), the relevant document text and/or images are sent to Google's Gemini (Generative Language) API for processing, and a result is returned to the App. This happens only when you invoke an AI feature. Google processes this data under Google's terms and privacy policy. We do not use AI output to build advertising or user profiles. AI features are optional.

6. On-Device Processing (OCR, Signatures, PDF, Images)

The following run locally on your device and do not transmit your content to us: text recognition (OCR) via on-device machine learning (Google ML Kit); electronic signatures you draw or type (stored in a local vault); PDF operations (create, merge, password/encryption protect, compress, split, annotate, view); image enhancement filters; and document (.docx) viewing.

7. Cloud Backup (Optional — Your Own Account)

You may optionally back up your documents to your own Google Drive or your own Dropbox account. If you connect an account, you authorize the App via OAuth to upload files and a backup manifest to a dedicated folder in your cloud storage. We do not store your documents on our servers or receive a copy of your backups. Google Drive access is limited to files the App creates (the drive.file scope); Dropbox access is limited to the App's scoped folder. You can disconnect at any time.

8. Local Storage & Security

Documents, extracted text, folders, and metadata are stored in a local database and your device's app storage; settings and usage counters are stored in local preferences. The Secure Folder encrypts files at rest with AES-256; the encryption key, PIN, and biometric settings are stored in the platform's secure keystore (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain). PDFs may be password-protected at your request. Network connections use HTTPS, and App Check verifies request integrity. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google / Firebase — Authentication, App Check, Cloud Messaging, Cloud Functions, and the Gemini AI API (policies.google.com/privacy).
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (revenuecat.com/privacy).
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — purchase processing.
  • Dropbox (only if you connect it) — cloud backup (dropbox.com/privacy).

10. Data Retention

Content you create is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Account information tied to sign-in is retained by Firebase for as long as your account exists (you may request deletion). Subscription records are retained by RevenueCat and the app stores as required for billing and legal purposes. Cloud backups remain in your own cloud account until you delete them.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

12. International Data Transfers

Some providers (including Google/Firebase, RevenueCat, and — if you connect it — Dropbox) may process data in the United States and other countries. Where personal data of users in the EEA/UK is transferred internationally, such providers rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses).

Account & data deletion. You can sign out at any time. To delete your account and associated account data, contact maafcoders@gmail.com and we will process your request. You can also delete documents within the App, disconnect cloud accounts, and remove all locally stored data by uninstalling the App.

GDPR (EEA/UK). Our legal bases are your consent (optional sign-in, AI features, cloud backup), performance of a contract (subscriptions you purchase), and our legitimate interests (securing and operating the App). You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing, the right to data portability, and the right to withdraw consent at any time, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

CCPA (California). In the preceding 12 months we do not "sell" or "share" personal information as defined by the CCPA, and we do not use it for cross-context behavioral advertising. California residents have the right to know, delete, and correct personal information, and to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

To exercise any right, contact maafcoders@gmail.com.
